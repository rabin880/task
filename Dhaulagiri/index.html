Introduction

Dhaulagiri Laghubitta Bittiya Sanstha Ltd. (DLBSL) is a micro finance institution licensed as a national level "D" class financial institution from Nepal Rastra Bank, the central bank of Nepal. Established on 3rd July 2018 as a Public Limited Company under Company Act 2063 of Nepal and licensed from the Central Bank of Nepal on 19th Feb 2019, its head office is at Balgung Municipality 2 Shreenagar Tole Baglung. It aims to provide banking services to the rural unbanked population and contribute to the overall poverty reduction program through increment of production and productivity of rural Nepal.

CEO's Message

It's my pleasure well-coming you to the website of Dhaulagiri laghubitta bittiya sanstha Limited (DLBS). DLBS is a micro finance financial institution dedicated to serve the deprived family. Our entire team member is committed towards economic improvement of the deprived community. All the branch of our institution are established in rural areas and micro finance services are delivered on those areas.

We are achieving very good financial result and operational performance in very short period through the hard working & dedicated team and advisory support from promoters & other stakeholders. We already have opened and established fifty eight branch networks in different district of the country. We are also planning to expand the micro finance service through the branch office additional district near future.

We thank you for your time to view our website and we would also like to thank all the stakeholder partners and regulation body for your valuable support and look forward to continuing as well as. Thank you.
